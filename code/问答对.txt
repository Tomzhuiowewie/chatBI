用户问题：查看川XN288N车辆5月13号的运动轨迹; 
SQL语句：SELECT p.* FROM sb_position p JOIN sb_device d ON p.device_code = d.device_code WHERE d.license_plate = '川XN288N' AND d.type = '01' AND STR_TO_DATE(CONCAT('2025-05-13', ' 00:00:00'), '%Y-%m-%d %H:%i:%s') <= p.trigger_time AND p.trigger_time <= STR_TO_DATE(CONCAT('2025-05-13', ' 23:59:59'), '%Y-%m-%d %H:%i:%s') LIMIT 1;
